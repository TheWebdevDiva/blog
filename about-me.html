Hi, I'm Michelle!

Perfection is boring. People spend most of their lives desperately trying to impress others. Many are in jobs that aren't the least bit fulfilling, or in relationships that aren't satisfying.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of a burnout when I decided what I was doing with my life was no longer working for me. I literally made the decision to walk away from the apartment I was renting, the draining relationship, and the toxic work environment.

It was easier than I thought to make that pivot. And I'm glad I did!

>Now I'm living life on my terms

Now that I'm making the conscious choice to strive towards the person whom I want to become, things are so much more satisfying. I'm less stressed, enjoy life and my career more, and I have more free time to just be ME!

With how much I was able to live more on purpose and turn my life around, I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from living a simple life as well, which is why I started this site!
